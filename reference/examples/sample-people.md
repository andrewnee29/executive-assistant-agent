### Commentary for the builder:

- **Transcription error patterns** are gold — "Phoebe" → "Amy Fieber" saves the user from correcting every single meeting
- **Relationship context** (who reports to whom, who works on what) helps the agent make sense of discussions
- **Disambiguation notes** for common names prevent confusion
- **Dates** (when someone joined, when they were first encountered) help with context decay
- Entries grow organically — first encounter is minimal, enriched over time as more meetings are processed
